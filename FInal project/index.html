Pictures from Home (1983–92) Using his mother and father as subject matter,
Sultan created Pictures from Home over the course of nearly a decade. The seriesdocuments
Sultan’s parents in their home in the San Fernando Valley and in their retirement community
in Palm Desert, California. Investigating personal identity, family relationships, and the
ideas surrounding home, these photographs reflect the artist’s fascination with storytelling
and the narratives that are communicated through text, image and time.
